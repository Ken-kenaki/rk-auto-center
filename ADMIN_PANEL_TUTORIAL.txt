1. LOGGING IN TO THE ADMIN PANEL
================================================================================
Step 1: Open your web browser and navigate to the admin portal:
        URL: https://yourdomain.com/login (or http://localhost:3000/login when testing locally)
Step 2: Enter your authorized Admin Email Address and Password.
Step 3: Click "Sign In to Admin Portal".
Step 4: Once authenticated, you will automatically be redirected to the Admin Dashboard.

================================================================================
2. MAIN DASHBOARD OVERVIEW (/dashboard)
================================================================================
The Dashboard home screen gives you a quick snapshot of business performance:
- Total Inventory Count: Total cars active for sale vs. sold/reserved.
- Monthly Leads: Total customer inquiries received this month via form/phone.
- Monthly Sales Volume: Revenue generated from car sales.
- Recent Inquiries Widget: Quick view of the latest customer messages requiring response.
- Quick Actions Menu: One-click buttons to "Add New Car" or "Log New Sale".

3. CAR INVENTORY MANAGEMENT (/dashboard/cars)
================================================================================
Manage all vehicles listed on the RK Auto Center marketplace.

3.1 Viewing All Cars:
    - Click "Inventory" or "Cars" in the left sidebar menu.
    - Use the search bar at the top to search by Brand, Model, Year, or Stock Number.
    - Filter listings by status: "Available", "Pending", or "Sold".

3.2 Adding a New Car Listing:
    - Step 1: Click the "+ Add New Vehicle" button at the top right of the Cars page.
    - Step 2: Fill in Basic Vehicle Information:
              * Make / Brand (e.g., Toyota, Volkswagen, Hyundai)
              * Model Name & Variant (e.g., Land Cruiser Prado TX)
              * Manufacturing Year (e.g., 2022)
              * Body Type (e.g., SUV, Sedan, Hatchback, Pickup Truck)
              * Condition (New, Used, Certified Pre-Owned)
    - Step 3: Enter Specs & Pricing:
              * Listing Price (in NPR / Rs.)
              * Mileage (Total Kilometers driven)
              * Fuel Type (Petrol, Diesel, Electric, Hybrid)
              * Transmission (Automatic / Manual)
              * Engine Capacity (CC or kW)
              * Registration Number & Ownership Details
    - Step 4: Upload High-Resolution Photos:
              * Click "Upload Images" and drag & drop vehicle photos.
              * Set the main primary thumbnail photo (First image in gallery).
    - Step 5: Vehicle Features & Description:
              * Check off features (e.g., Sunroof, Leather Seats, 360 Camera, ABS).
              * Write a detailed description for buyers.
    - Step 6: Click "Publish Vehicle" to list it immediately on the public website.

3.3 Editing an Existing Car Listing:
    - Find the vehicle card or row in the inventory list.
    - Click the "Edit" (pencil icon) button.
    - Update prices, add/remove photos, or change specs.
    - Click "Save Changes".

3.4 Deleting / Archiving a Car:
    - Click the "Delete" (trash icon) button on a vehicle row.
    - Confirm the prompt: Select "Archive" to hide from public view, or "Permanent Delete".

4. LEADS & CUSTOMER ENQUIRIES MANAGEMENT (/dashboard/leads)
================================================================================
Track incoming phone calls, WhatsApp requests, trade-in forms, and contact inquiries.

4.1 Viewing & Filtering Leads:
    - Click "Leads" in the sidebar menu.
    - View leads sorted chronologically (Newest first).
    - Filter by Lead Type: "General Inquiry", "Test Drive Request", "Sell/Trade-In Request", or "Financing".

4.2 Updating Lead Status:
    - Click on any lead row to open the Lead Details drawer.
    - Change status dropdown:
      * "New" -> Unread message.
      * "In Contact" -> Sales agent has reached out.
      * "Scheduled Visit" -> Customer visiting showroom.
      * "Converted" -> Deal closed.
      * "Closed / Lost" -> Customer not interested.

4.3 Adding Contact Notes:
    - Under the customer profile, type internal team notes (e.g., "Client preferred white interior, calling back Thursday").
    - Click "Save Note".

================================================================================
5. SALES & TRANSACTIONS MANAGEMENT (/dashboard/sales)
================================================================================
Record completed vehicle sales and track financial records.

5.1 Recording a New Sale:
    - Click "Sales" in the left sidebar menu.
    - Click "+ Record New Sale".
    - Select the Vehicle from the active inventory list (This marks the vehicle as "SOLD" automatically).
    - Enter Buyer Information (Name, Phone Number, Citizenship ID/Pan).
    - Enter Final Sale Price, Payment Mode (Cash, Bank Transfer, Finance), and Date.
    - Click "Submit Sale".

5.2 Generating Invoice / Receipt:
    - On any completed sale row, click "Download Receipt / Invoice".
    - Print or send the generated PDF directly to the buyer via email or WhatsApp.

================================================================================
6. CLIENT TESTIMONIALS MANAGEMENT (/dashboard/testimonials)
================================================================================
Manage customer reviews and feedback shown on the homepage and about page.

6.1 Adding / Approving Testimonials:
    - Click "Testimonials" in the sidebar menu.
    - Click "+ Add Testimonial".
    - Enter Client Name, Rating (1 to 5 stars), Review Text, and Client Photo.
    - Toggle "Show on Homepage" to feature it.
    - Click "Save".

================================================================================
7. SYSTEM SETTINGS & CUSTOMIZATION (/dashboard/settings)
================================================================================
Configure global site details and security settings.

7.1 Site Information & Contact Details:
    - Go to "Settings" -> "General".
    - Update Showroom Phone Numbers, Email Address, Facebook Page URL, and Showroom Hours.
    - Click "Update General Settings".

7.2 Admin User Accounts & Security:
    - Go to "Settings" -> "Team & Users".
    - Click "Add New Admin" to invite team members.
    - Change current admin password under "Security".
